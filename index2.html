The Day in the Life of an Indian Woman

Women everyday in India live in fear of going to work, going to school, and enjoying their livelihood due to the growing number of rapes and sexual assaults. Follow the story of a young woman, dealing with everyday life in India.

Jyoti is a 25-year-old woman, living in New Dheli, India. She works for a marketing firm 10 miles away from her home. She lives with one female roommate, who works at a diner and goes to school part-time.


Every morning, Jyoti wakes up at 5:30am to make it on time to work at 8am. She brushes her teeth, eats a light breakfast,and gets dressed. Knowing her job is a bit of a journey from her home, she considers what would be the best thing to wear.

What should Jyoti Wear?

While this is beneficial due to the intense heat in India, Jyoti is worried of the unrequited attention she typically recieves when dressed this way.In a survey done by the Thomas Reuter Foundation, India was found to be the most dangerous country for women. Jyoti was always told by her mother to stay covered up to keep herself safe.
