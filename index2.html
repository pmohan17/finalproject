Walking a Mile in Her Shoes

According to the National Crime Bureau 92 women are reportedly raped each day in India. Follow the fictional story of a girl named Puja, as she lives through a single day in India.

Puja is a 25-year-old woman, living in New Dheli, India. She works for a marketing firm 10 miles away from her home. She lives with one female roommate, who works at a diner and goes to school part-time.
